AirSensor Project

The project aims to develop a network of plug-and-play IoT sensors for real-time air quality monitoring, detecting pollutants such as particulate matter (PM10, PM2.5) and harmful gases (NO2, CO, CO2, VOC) to provide accurate and timely data on ambient air quality.
